Stargate

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. I am a huge fan of my family's country of origin, Egypt. The only thing I absolutely hate about this movies is the alien aspect. However, it was a compelling tale so I will let it slide.

This movie follows the life of Dr. Daniel Jackson, a disgraced Egyptologist for his on the fringe theories. However it is these theories that lead to the discover of another planet and humans and figures that appear to be the ancient Egyptian gods. A fantastical ride on the auspices of science and trying to unfold one of the still greatest mysteries known to man, the formation of the Great Pyramid. The movie truly is an epic fantastic and fun film.

This movie stars Kurt Russell, James Spader and a young Djimon Hounsou.
