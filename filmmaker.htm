Experience As A Filmmaker

I have had alot of different experiences with film. I have had the pleasure of working as an actor in a few films and TV shows, including SMOKETOWN , (notably episodes 5 and 6), which was shot here in Louisville. I have a long career of acting in plays for the stage, but my passion is creating them. I have written, directed, produced, acted and edited several plays over the last 7 years. My desire is to create short films and enter film festivals.
