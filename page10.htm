Scott Pilgrim vs The World

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. This movie spoke to the angst of a generation while also parodying it at the same time. The movie was brilliant in execution and I am a complete fan of every movie done by Edgar Wright.

This movie follows the life of young slacker Scott Pilgrim who seems to be going nowhere fast. Playing off of his infatuation with his newest girlfriend, Ramona Flowers, he gets lured into a battle with the League of Evil Exes. The wacky video game and comic book-esque nature of the movie kicked things into high gear and added the perfect backdrop for this love tale gone wrong.

This movie stars Michael Cera, Kieran Culkin, Mary Elizabeth Winstead, Jason Schwartz, Anna Kendrick and a truly young all star cast.
