The Matrix

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. Incredible action and a sci-fi story that was unheard of on top of eye dropping special effects that challenged what you thought you knew about the world and technology since the scourge of Skynet in Terminator 2.

This movie follows the life of Mr. Thomas A. Anderson, otherwise known as Neo, who is destined to change and/or save the world. The problem is can one truly be a hero if they don't believe that they are a hero themself. Guided by the infamous Morpheus and Trinity, Neo recognizes that he is more powerful than he has ever imagined.

This movie stars Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, Carrie-Anne Moss and Hugo Weaving, and was the first mega hit in a trilogy of Matrix titles for the Wachowskis, who are finishing shooting The Matrix 4 now after a 18 year hiatus.
