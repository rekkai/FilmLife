The Usual Suspects

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. A classic who-dun it that has you guessing at every turn. Truly unpredictable and taught, thrilling joy ride in a hail of bullets and mental chess.

This movie follows an unlikely group of criminals who all met in a criminal line-up at the police department. Before they all know it, they are being accused of a crime offending one of the biggest criminals in the known world even though he is a complete enigma to everyone. Filled with intrigue, drama and a bait and switch twist that will leave people baffled, this star studded cast delivered on the mystery.

This movie stars Gabriel Byrne, Chazz Palmintieri, Benicio Del Toro, Kevin Pollak, Stephen Baldwin and Kevin Spacey, who delivers a fantastic performance as the handi-capable criminal, Verbal Kent.
