Rekkai's Film Life

I love EVERYTHING about film!

Iâ€™ve been an extreme film buff since I was a child. My father had an affinity for Betamax. We loved watching all of the movies we could and being transported away into an engrossing story. But I evolved to the point where not only do I collect them, but now I want to be in them. In front of the camera, behind the camera and even write them.

I have divided this page into several sections, which include My Favorite Films, Experience as a Filmmaker and Passion for Writing.

My Favorite Films

In order to stay informed about the latest movies, I utilize one of my favorite websites The Internet Movie Database to fill in all of the blanks in my memory.

IMDB contains all of the imformation about past, present and future movies and TV shows.

My collection at one point was EXTENSIVE! How extensive? I owned over 7,000 movies in VHS, DVD and Blu-ray format. I was an avid collector and that gave me a chance to develop a true sense of what I liked and the type of filmmaker I would be one day. To the side are 10 of my favorite films but it is due to what they meant to me, as opposed to if I think they are the best movies of all time.

Experience As A Filmmaker

I have had alot of different experiences with film. I have had the pleasure of working as an actor in a few films and TV shows, including SMOKETOWN , (notably episodes 5 and 6), which was shot here in Louisville. I have a long career of acting in plays for the stage, but my passion is creating them. I have written, directed, produced, acted and edited several plays over the last 7 years. My desire is to create short films and enter film festivals.

Passion For Writing

I have been a writer since middle school. In high school, I was hired by Disney to be a writer and like an idiot, I did not take the position because I was convinced I was an athlete. I wasn't aware that I could be more than one thing. I was dead set on being a college athlete, of which I was successful, but it was after a stint in the U.S. Army and a tour of the middle east. I only wrote poems and journaled at this time. I spent time imagining writing textbooks and tomes to improve schools and systems before I ventured in to writing novels and short stories. I eventually made my way to plays and scripts and search my calendar for more time to read and more time to write.
