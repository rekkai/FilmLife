My Favorite Films

In order to stay informed about the latest movies, I utilize one of my favorite websites The Internet Movie Database to fill in all of the blanks in my memory.

IMDB contains all of the imformation about past, present and future movies and TV shows.

My collection at one point was EXTENSIVE! How extensive? I owned over 7,000 movies in VHS, DVD and Blu-ray format. I was an avid collector and that gave me a chance to develop a true sense of what I liked and the type of filmmaker I would be one day.
