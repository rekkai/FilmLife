Passion For Writing

I have been a writer since middle school. In high school, I was hired by Disney to be a writer and like an idiot, I did not take the position because I was convinced I was an athlete. I wasn't aware that I could be more than one thing. I was dead set on being a college athlete, of which I was successful, but it was after a stint in the U.S. Army and a tour of the middle east. I only wrote poems and journaled at this time. I spent time imagining writing textbooks and tomes to improve schools and systems before I ventured in to writing novels and short stories. I eventually made my way to plays and scripts and search my calendar for more time to read and more time to write.
