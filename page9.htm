Raiders Of The Lost Ark

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. Being already a fan of two movies Star Wars movies, another movies starring Han Solo, aka Harrison Ford was a very welcome cinema experience. And that it was. I fancied myself a world traveler and to see someone globe trotting and tomb raiding in some of the most insane adventures was a delight for any young person.

This movie follows the exploits of Dr. Henry "Indiana" Jones, Jr., an archaelogist and professor whose travels take him all over the world to find treasure to put on display at his University. This movie has him in search of one of the most mythical treasures of all time, the lost Ark of the Covenant, written about in the Bible to contain great power. Indiana Jones adventures were the beginning of a worldwide favorite franchise.

This movie stars Harrison Ford, John Rhys-Davies, Karen Allen and a young Alfred Molina.
