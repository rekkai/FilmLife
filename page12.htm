Dogma

CLICK ON THE PICTURE TO SEE THE TRAILER FOR THE MOVIE

I love this movie for a number of reasons. I love Kevin Smith movies. Personal preference. I love that he puts all of his friends in his movies, even if for a small part. I like that he is a self professed nerd but is also an accomplished writer of comic books, TV shows and movies, an actor, Director, Producer and Entrepreneur who seems to still be himself but in a different tax bracket.

This movie follows the adventure and journey of the last scion and two fallen angels, Azrael and Loki. The movie is based on the premise of an alleged loophole in Catholic Dogma that would allow the angels who have been banished to Earth to return to Heaven, however that would unmake all of reality by proving God wrong. The movie was HIGHLY controversial, yet thought provoking and was condemned by many before seeing a single fram which kind of proves the point of blind zeal.

This movie stars Linda Fiorentino, George Carlin, Ben Affleck, Matt Damon, Chris Rock, Alan Rickman, Kevin Smith, Salma Hayek, and many more stars and Kevin Smith's friends and characters from other movies.
